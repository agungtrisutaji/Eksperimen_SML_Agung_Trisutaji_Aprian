Preprocessing summary:

1. Load the Telco Customer Churn dataset from `telco_customer_churn_raw/`.
2. Convert `TotalCharges` to numeric.
3. Drop `customerID`.
4. Map `Churn`: `No -> 0`, `Yes -> 1`.
5. Split train and test data with stratification.
6. Fit preprocessing only on training data.
7. Use median imputation and standard scaling for numeric features.
8. Use most-frequent imputation and one-hot encoding for categorical features.
9. Save processed train/test data, feature names, preprocessing summary, and preprocessor artifact.
