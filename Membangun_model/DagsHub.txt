# DagsHub and MLflow Tracking

Expected MLflow Tracking URI:

```text
https://dagshub.com/agungtrisutaji/Membangun-Sistem-Machine-Learning.mlflow
```

Use environment variables only:

```text
DAGSHUB_USERNAME=agungtrisutaji
DAGSHUB_TOKEN=<your token>
MLFLOW_TRACKING_URI=https://dagshub.com/agungtrisutaji/Membangun-Sistem-Machine-Learning.mlflow
```

Do not commit tokens, passwords, `.env`, or printed credential values.
